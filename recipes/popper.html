Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Stuffed Jalapenos with cream cheese and bacon!

Prep time: 20 minutes / Cook time: 45 minutes / Total time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Servings: 12 / Yield: 24 stuffed jalapenos

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 1 package of whipped cream cheese
  • 12 slices of bacon, cut in half

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Stuff each jalapeno half with whipped cream cheese. Wrap a half slice of bacon around each stuffed jalapeno half
  3. Arrange wrapped jalapeno halves in a single layer on a medium baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until bacon is evenly browned and crisp
